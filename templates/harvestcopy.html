Smart ikigega short description

we help farmers to store their harvesting data in digital way as well as helping them to get direct loan on their phones in three categories : Firstly we help them to get their harvesting record directly on their phone secondly our technology will help them to get direct loan on their phone depending on their harvesting quantity lastly we will help them to get their harvesting insurance.to help them to recover .

working system

we are the interaction between farmers and their customers especiallyfarmers who works in the cooperatives as we helpingthem to get direct notification on their phones after bringing their harvesting to the companies who buy from them . not only interacting between farmers and their customers. but also we will help them to get direct loan in time they want it through their phones depending on their harvesting quantity.
